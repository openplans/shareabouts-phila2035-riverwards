The Riverwards consist of a number of Philadelphia neighborhoods, including:

  • Fishtown
  • Olde Richmond
  • Port Richmond
  • East Kensington
  • Kensington
  • Bridesburg

Each neighborhood has its own distinct character and assets. Together, they form a diverse and vibrant District within the City of Philadelphia.

Some Demographics of the District include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Total population is 68,489 people
  • The largest age cohort, ages 20 to 44, comprises 39% of the population
  • Homeownership rate of 61%
  • Poverty rate of 31%
  • 14 Bus routes connect the River Wards to the rest of the City

    • Source: U.S. Census Bureau; Census 2010; 2008-2012 ACS Data; using American FactFinder
